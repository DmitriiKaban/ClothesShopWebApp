Delivery

Choose any country!

Our company "Bertha" can deliver your order right to a post office near your house in any country of the world! This is why our online shop is so unique: everyone regardless of their place of residence has an opportunity to buy modern, fashionable and branded clothes from shorts for long summer evenings to coats for a winter walk.

The process

To make sure your order will be delivered to your home, enter the name of the country, city and your home address correctly. You will receive an email with the whole information about your order(better check that one more time). After this all you have to do is to wait until your clothes will arrive and then wear them with a great pleasure! Thanks for your shopping with "Bertha"!

About us

Foundation

Our company was founded in 2022, Moldova, Chisinau. The idea of starting an online shop crossed our minds when we were going to order some clothes from "ASOS". We were not pleased with the provided service. We decided to make people's life better by creating an alternative online shop with a lange variety of products from coats to shorts.

The idea of the name "Bertha" came from a famous novel "The Witcher", our creator was a fan of this universe.
